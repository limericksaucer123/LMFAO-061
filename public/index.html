L.M.F.A.O

This is an independent media organisation seperated from the hate-filled algorithmic hell that is modern social media and news. We aim to uplift Irish creatives through honest and passionate coverage of their work and national/local news.

This website acts as a centralized source of articles and investigative pieces produced by our members. The topics include news , famous cases of high strangeness , crime , musings on local issues , interviews with musicians/bands , album reviews , gig guides , cults , scene reports , investigative journalism , monsters , wolfmen , cryptids of all sorts etc. We accept submissions of all forms : articles , prose , short stories, poems , paintings , mp3 files , pieces of paper , email , letter. If you believe the masses must see it then we will post it.
